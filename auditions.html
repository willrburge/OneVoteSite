CASTING CALL: One Vote! The Musical

An Original Musical by Larry Groce and Todd Burge

Location: Marietta, Ohio Performance: July 2026 Union Status: Non-Union Executive Producer: Northwest Territory Museum Society

One Vote! – The Musical tells the story of the New England settlers who founded Marietta, Ohio, and how events between 1786–1820 helped shape the young United States. The story features historical figures such as Rufus Putnam and Ephraim Cutler, whose single vote helped secure Ohio’s entry into the Union as a free state, alongside compelling fictional characters including the lead female role Abigail Worthy.

Blending pop, rock, Americana, and folk, the musical explores the lives of frontier settlers while also examining the experiences of Native peoples and African Americans during this pivotal time in American history.

Casting Notes Seeking strong singer-actors of all backgrounds for principal and ensemble roles. We are especially seeking Native American performers (male and female) for significant roles in the production.

Submission Requirements • Headshot and resume • Singing reel (required) • Acting reel (required) • Dance reel (if available)
